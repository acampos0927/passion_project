The Prolific Story of Joe Mad

Joe Mad has made a career from his art by applying it to his passion for comics and video games

His broad work in comics ranges from Marvel's Uncanny X-men in the 90s to DCs justice league in the modern era.

He broke into video game in the early 2000s with an indie title but didn't see major strides until the release of his triple A title known as "Darksiders"
